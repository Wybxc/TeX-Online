\paragraph*{1} ~

\begin{algorithm}[ht]
    \renewcommand{\algorithmicrequire}{\textbf{Input:}}
    \renewcommand{\algorithmicensure}{\textbf{Output:}}
    \caption{Remove duplicate elements in decrement sequence}
    \begin{algorithmic}[1]        
        \Require A decrement sequence $A=\{a_i\}$ of length $n$, $i=0,1,\ldots, n-1$.
        \State $i \gets 0$, $j \gets 0$
        \While{$i < n$}
        \State $A_j \gets A_i$
        \While{$i < n$\And$A_i = A_j$}
        \State $i \gets i + 1$
        \EndWhile
        \State $j \gets j + 1$
        \EndWhile
        \Ensure Sequence $\{A_0, A_1,\ldots, A_j\}$        
    \end{algorithmic}
\end{algorithm}

时间复杂度: 内层循环中 $i$ 每次自增 1, 最大值小于 $n$, 故内层循环执行
$n$ 次, 外层循环执行不超过 $n$ 次, 总时间复杂度为 $O(n)$.

\paragraph*{2} ~

\begin{algorithm}[ht]
    \renewcommand{\algorithmicrequire}{\textbf{Input:}}
    \renewcommand{\algorithmicensure}{\textbf{Output:}}
    \caption{Reverse a linked list}
    \begin{algorithmic}[1]
        \Require A linked list $L$ and its head node $head$.
        \State $p \gets head.next$
        \Comment{$p$ is the first node of $L$}
        \State $q \gets$ \nil
        \While{$p$ is not \nil}
        \State $r \gets p.next$
        \State $p.next \gets q$
        \State $q \gets p$
        \State $p \gets r$
        \EndWhile
        \State $head.next \gets q$
        \Ensure The head node of reversed linked list is $head$.
    \end{algorithmic}
\end{algorithm}

时间复杂度: 循环遍历链表的每一个节点, 且每个节点只访问一遍, 因此时间复杂度为$O(n)$.

空间复杂度: 仅使用了常数个变量, 因此空间复杂度为$O(1)$.

\paragraph*{3} ~

\begin{algorithm}[ht]
    \renewcommand{\algorithmicrequire}{\textbf{Input:}}
    \renewcommand{\algorithmicensure}{\textbf{Output:}}
    \caption{Determine whether a linked list is cyclic}
    \begin{algorithmic}[1]
        \Require A linked list $L$ and its head node $head$.
        \State $p \gets head.next$
        \Comment{The slow pointer}
        \State $q \gets head.next$
        \Comment{The fast pointer}
        \While{$p$ is not \nil \And $q$ is not \nil}
        \State $p \gets p.next$
        \State $q \gets q.next$
        \If{$q$ is not \nil}
        \State $q \gets q.next$
        \EndIf
        \If{$p = q$}
        \State \Return True
        \EndIf
        \EndWhile
        \State \Return False
    \end{algorithmic}
\end{algorithm}

时间复杂度: 链表的每个节点至多在循环中被访问两遍, 因此时间复杂度为$O(n)$.

空间复杂度: 仅使用了常数个变量, 因此空间复杂度为$O(1)$.
